Copy/Paste Clipboard Ace

Privacy Policy & Support

Privacy Policy

Copy/Paste Clipboard Ace is designed with privacy in mind.

Data Collection

The app stores clipboard history locally on your device. No data is transmitted, collected, or shared with any third party.

Local Processing

Clipboard content may be processed locally on your device to enable features such as search and content organization. This processing never leaves your device.

User Control

  • You can pause clipboard monitoring at any time
  • You can clear all clipboard history
  • You can enable Private Mode to temporarily stop saving items

Sensitive Information

The app includes an option to skip saving sensitive content such as passwords or credit card information. Users are encouraged to keep this setting enabled.

Permissions

The app may request Accessibility permission to enable automatic pasting. This is optional and used only when you choose to enable it.
