Copy/Paste Clipboard Ace

Privacy Policy

Overview

Copy/Paste Clipboard Ace is designed with privacy in mind. Clipboard data stays on your Mac and is not uploaded or shared.

Data Storage

The app stores clipboard history locally on your device. No clipboard data is transmitted to external servers.

Local Processing

Clipboard content may be processed locally on your device to enable features such as search, tagging, and organization. This processing never leaves your device.

User Control

  • You can pause clipboard monitoring at any time.
  • You can clear clipboard history.
  • You can disable automatic monitoring.
  • You can use Private Mode to temporarily stop saving copied items.

Sensitive Content

The app includes an option to skip saving sensitive data such as passwords, credit card numbers, and social security numbers. This feature is enabled by default but may not detect all sensitive content.

Permissions

The app may request Accessibility permission to enable automatic pasting. This is optional and only used when you choose to enable it.
